What is this page?

Tools & Equipment tracks every piece of equipment in your bakery — mixers, cake pans, piping bags, decorating turntables, everything. Recipes are linked to the tools they require.

Why Track Tools?

  • Recipes linked to tools show on the Prep Sheet so bakers know what equipment to pull
  • Helps you spot when a tool is needed for multiple same-day orders (scheduling conflicts)
  • Tracks quantity — if you have 3 round pans, you know you can bake 3 cakes simultaneously

Managing Equipment

  • Add every major piece of equipment with its quantity and location in the kitchen
  • Mark tools inactive if they’re broken or out for repair so they don’t show in recipe requirements
  • Use the notes field for maintenance reminders (e.g., “serviced annually”)
Link tools to recipes when you set them up — the Prep Sheet will then include an equipment checklist for each day’s production run.
