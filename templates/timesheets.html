What is this page?

This page shows the complete clock-in/clock-out history for one employee. Use it to review hours worked, approve timesheets, and calculate pay.

Reading the Timesheet

  • Clock In / Clock Out — exact times the employee started and ended their shift
  • Hours — calculated automatically (minus any break time)
  • Source — whether they clocked in via web, mobile, or QR code
  • Location — if GPS was captured at clock-in (mobile/QR), confirms they were on-site

Approving & Payroll

  • Review entries at the end of each pay period
  • Check for missing clock-outs (employee forgot) and correct manually if needed
  • Total hours × hourly rate (set on the employee profile) = wages owed

Fixing Errors

  • If an employee forgot to clock out, their shift will show as still open — edit the record and add a clock-out time
Run a timesheet review every Friday before weekend shifts start — it’s easier to fix errors while the week is fresh.
