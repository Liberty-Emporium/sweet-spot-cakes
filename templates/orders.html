What is this page?

This is your master order list — every cake, cupcake, and custom order in the system. This is where you track what needs to be made and what needs to go out the door.

Order Statuses (the lifecycle)

  • Pending — customer placed order, not yet reviewed
  • Confirmed — you’ve accepted it, team knows it’s coming
  • In Production — actively being baked/decorated
  • Ready — finished and waiting for pickup
  • Delivered — customer has it, order complete
  • Cancelled — won’t be fulfilled

How to Move an Order Forward

  • Click View → on any order to open the detail page
  • On the detail page, use the status dropdown to advance it
  • Add items, collect payment, and mark paid in full from there

Filtering & Search

  • Use the status pills at the top to quickly see just Pending or Ready orders
  • Search by customer name or order number in the search box
Start every morning by clicking “Pending” to review and confirm overnight orders before baking starts.
