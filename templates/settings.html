What is this page?

Settings controls the configuration for the entire bakery app — your business name, tax rate, email settings, admin password, and integrations like Stripe and Square.

What to Configure First

  • Bakery Name — appears on receipts, menus, and the login screen
  • Tax Rate — applied automatically to all orders (enter as a decimal, e.g., 0.075 for 7.5%)
  • Admin Email — where order notifications and system emails go
  • Admin Password — change this from the default immediately

Payment Integrations

  • Stripe — add your Stripe secret key to enable online payments and payment links on orders
  • Square — add your Square access token and location ID to process in-person card payments

Email (SMTP)

  • Configure your Gmail or SMTP credentials so the app can send order receipts to customers
  • Use a Gmail App Password — not your regular Gmail password

User Management

  • Add or deactivate staff login accounts (separate from employee PIN logins)
  • Staff accounts are for managers and admins who need full app access
Set your tax rate and bakery name before taking any orders — these appear on every receipt you send to customers.
