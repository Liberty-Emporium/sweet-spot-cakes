What is this page?

The VIP Loyalty Club keeps your best customers coming back. Members earn points with every order and get access to exclusive specials and birthday offers.

How Customers Join

  • They visit your public /join page (link in sidebar) and sign up
  • You can also manually add them from this page
  • Every customer with an order in the system can be enrolled

Managing Specials

  • Click + Add Special to create a discount or promotion for VIP members
  • Set a valid date range — expired specials deactivate automatically
  • Specials appear on the public menu and join page

Birthday Marketing

  • The Birthdays This Month section shows members with birthdays coming up
  • Use this to proactively reach out with a birthday cake offer — it’s one of the highest-converting outreach moments

Driving Sales with Loyalty

  • Members who feel valued order more often and refer friends
  • Use the Marketing page to send campaigns specifically to VIP members
Tell every walk-in customer about the VIP club and hand them the /join QR code. Even 20 loyal members can significantly boost monthly revenue.
