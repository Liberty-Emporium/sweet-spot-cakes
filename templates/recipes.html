What is this page?

Recipes are your product catalog. Every cake, cupcake, and item you sell lives here. Recipes are added to orders and appear on your public Menu page.

What a Recipe Contains

  • Name & Category — how it appears on the menu and in orders
  • Base Price — the starting price added to orders automatically
  • Ingredients — linked to inventory so stock levels update when orders are placed
  • Tools needed — what equipment the baker needs
  • Prep & Bake time — helps with scheduling and the prep sheet

How Recipes Drive Sales

  • Only active recipes appear on the public menu and in the order form — deactivate anything you’re not currently making
  • Accurate prices here mean orders are priced correctly from the start
  • Keep descriptions and photos updated — they show on the customer-facing menu

Adding a New Recipe

  • Click + New Recipe, fill in name, category, price, and description
  • Add ingredients from inventory — this links your stock to production
  • Set it to Active when ready to sell
Set accurate prep + bake times on every recipe — the Prep Sheet uses them to estimate kitchen workload for the day.
