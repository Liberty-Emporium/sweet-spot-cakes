What is this page?

The Prep Sheet is your daily production plan. It rolls up every order due today and tomorrow into a single list of what to bake, how much of each ingredient you need, and what equipment to pull.

How to Use It Every Morning

  • Open the Prep Sheet at the start of each shift — it auto-generates from today’s confirmed orders
  • Print it or keep it open on a tablet in the kitchen
  • Check off items as they’re completed

What’s on the Sheet

  • Orders due today — customer name, item, pickup time
  • Total ingredients needed — aggregated across all orders so you pull once
  • Equipment needed — based on recipe tool requirements
  • Estimated time — total prep + bake time so you know when to start

Getting Orders Out on Time

  • Work backwards from the earliest pickup time — if a cake needs 3 hours and pickup is at noon, start by 9 AM
  • If the ingredient list shows something you’re low on, check Inventory and reorder or substitute immediately
Print the Prep Sheet and post it in the kitchen every morning. Your bakers shouldn’t need to log into the app just to know what to make.
