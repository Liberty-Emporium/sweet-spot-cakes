What is this page?

Track every dollar going out of the business — ingredients, packaging, equipment, rent, utilities, marketing spend. Expenses feed directly into your profit reports.

Logging an Expense

  • Click + Add Expense
  • Enter the amount, category, vendor, and date
  • Add a description so you remember what it was for
  • Log expenses as they happen — don’t let them pile up

Categories to Use

  • Ingredients — flour, sugar, dairy, eggs, etc.
  • Packaging — boxes, ribbon, bags, labels
  • Equipment — pans, mixers, tools
  • Marketing — social ads, printing, signage
  • Utilities/Rent — overhead
  • Labor — contractor or extra help

Why This Matters

  • The Reports page subtracts expenses from revenue to show your real profit
  • Knowing your costs helps you price orders correctly — never guess on cake pricing
  • Tax time is much easier when expenses are already categorized
