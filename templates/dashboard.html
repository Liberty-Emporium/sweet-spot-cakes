What is this page?

The Dashboard is your home base. It gives you a live snapshot of everything happening in the bakery right now — orders, revenue, stock levels, and who’s clocked in.

The 6 Stat Tiles

  • Orders Today — how many new orders came in today
  • Pending — orders waiting to be confirmed
  • Ready for Pickup — finished cakes waiting on customers
  • Revenue (7 days) — total payments collected this week
  • Low Stock — ingredients below reorder level (red = act now)
  • Staff Clocked In — how many employees are on the clock

Getting Orders Out the Door

  • Check Upcoming Pickups — these are orders due soon. Click any to view or update it.
  • Click + New Order to start a fresh order immediately.
  • Red low-stock items mean you might not be able to fill upcoming orders — restock or adjust recipes.

Daily Routine

  • Morning: Check pending orders, confirm any overnights, check low stock.
  • Midday: Move in-production orders to Ready when done.
  • Close: Verify all pickups were collected or rescheduled.
