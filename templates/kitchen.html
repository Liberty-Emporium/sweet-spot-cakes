What is this page?

The Kitchen Queue is the baker’s view. It shows every active order that needs to be made, sorted by pickup date so the most urgent work is at the top.

How Bakers Use This Page

  • Check this page at the start of every shift to see what’s due
  • Orders show customer name, pickup date/time, items ordered, and special notes
  • Tap an order to see full details including flavors, decorations, and allergy notes
  • When a cake is finished and boxed, change its status to Ready

Priority Order

  • Today’s pickups first — always start with orders due today
  • Confirmed orders are approved and ready to bake
  • In Production means someone has already started — don’t duplicate work

Getting the Product Out

  • Baker claims an order by moving it to In Production
  • When done: box it, label it with customer name + pickup time, move to Ready
  • Place it in the pickup area and notify the front staff
Pull the Prep Sheet every morning (sidebar → Prep Sheet) — it tells you exactly what ingredients you need for today’s orders.
