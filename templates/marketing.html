What is this page?

Create and manage marketing campaigns to bring in new orders and re-engage existing customers. Run birthday blasts, seasonal promos, and VIP-only offers from here.

Creating a Campaign

  • Click + New Campaign and give it a name and goal (e.g., “Mother’s Day Promo”)
  • Choose your audience: all customers, VIP members only, or birthday customers this month
  • Write your message — keep it short: what’s the offer and how do they order?
  • Use Generate Ad Copy to have AI write the message for you

Sending Campaigns

  • Review the recipient list before sending — make sure you’re targeting the right people
  • Campaigns send via email to customers who have an email address on file
  • Mark campaigns as Sent so you can track what went out and when

What Works

  • Birthday offers — highest open rate of any campaign type
  • Holiday specials — Valentine’s, Mother’s Day, Christmas — order 2 weeks ahead
  • Flash sales — “This weekend only” creates urgency
  • New item launches — tell your existing customers first
Run at least one campaign per month. Even a simple “what are you celebrating this month?” email keeps your bakery top of mind.
