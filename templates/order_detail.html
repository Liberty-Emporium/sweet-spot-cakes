What is this page?

This is the full detail view for one order. Everything you do to an order — adding items, collecting payment, changing status, sending receipts — happens here.

The Order Workflow

  • Add Items — Search recipes and add them to the order. Quantities and prices calculate automatically.
  • Collect Deposit — Use the payment section to record a partial payment (cash, card, or Stripe).
  • Update Status — Move the order through: Confirmed → In Production → Ready → Delivered.
  • Collect Balance — When customer picks up, collect the remaining balance and mark Paid in Full.
  • Mark Delivered — Final step. Closes the order.

Getting Paid

  • Use Record Payment to log cash or manual card payments
  • Use Charge with Stripe to send a payment link or charge a card online
  • The Balance Due field updates automatically as payments come in

Sending a Receipt

  • Enter the customer’s email and hit Send Receipt to email them a summary
Change status to “Ready” as soon as the cake is boxed up — it triggers the dashboard alert so staff know to call the customer.
