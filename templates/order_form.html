What is this page?

Use this form every time a customer places an order — in person, by phone, or online. Fill it out completely so the kitchen knows exactly what to make.

Step-by-Step

  • 1. Customer Info — Name, phone, and email. If they’re in the system, their info will auto-populate.
  • 2. Pickup Date & Time — Critical! This drives the kitchen schedule. Always confirm with the customer.
  • 3. Order Type — Custom cake, cupcakes, etc.
  • 4. Special Notes — Flavor, colors, text on cake, allergies, anything the baker needs to know.
  • 5. Submit — Creates the order as “Pending.” Go to the order detail next to add items and collect a deposit.

After Submitting

  • You’ll land on the Order Detail page — add the specific items (recipes) from there
  • Collect a deposit payment right away if possible
  • Change status to Confirmed once deposit is received
Always enter a pickup time, not just a date — it helps staff prioritize same-day orders.
